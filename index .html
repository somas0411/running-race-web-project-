The Course

A City-Wide Route

From the iconic Marina Beach sands to the quiet coves of Muttukadu — the APEX RUN course is both iconic and unforgettable.

Common Questions

FAQ

Is there an age minimum for the marathon? +
Yes. The 5K is open to all ages. 10K requires a minimum age of 14, the Half Marathon 16, and the Full Marathon 18. Participants under 18 must have a signed parent/guardian consent form.
What does the registration fee include? +
All registrations include a race bib with RFID chip timing, an official APEX RUN 2026 T-shirt, finisher medal, access to the race expo and pasta party, hydration stations along the route, and post-race refreshments at the finish village.
Can I transfer or defer my registration? +
Yes. Transfers to another participant are allowed until 31 March 2026 with a ₹200 admin fee. Deferrals to APEX RUN 2027 are available up to 7 days before race day for a ₹300 fee. No refunds after registration.
Where do I collect my race bib? +
Bibs must be collected in person at the Race Expo on 18 April 2026, held at Nandanam Exhibition Grounds between 10 AM and 7 PM. No race-day bib collection will be available. Bring your registration confirmation and a photo ID.
Is the course AIMS-certified? +
Yes. The Full Marathon course is AIMS and Athletics Federation of India (AFI) certified. Finishing times from the full marathon are eligible for use as qualification times for international events such as the Boston Marathon and Abbott World Marathon Majors.
